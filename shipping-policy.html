Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

We keep shipping simple: order today, and your Lutera is on its way fast.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday–Friday, excluding U.S. holidays). You’ll receive a confirmation email as soon as your order ships, including a tracking number.

Shipping Rates & Delivery

Standard shipping within the United States is a flat $5.99. The Buy 3 Get 2 FREE bundle ships free with Priority Shipping. Most orders arrive within 4–9 business days after processing.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, track it any time on our Track Order page using your order number and email address.

Address Changes

Need to fix a typo or change your delivery address? Reach out through our Contact page within 12 hours of placing your order and we’ll update it before it ships.

Lost, Delayed, or Damaged Packages

If your package is marked delivered but hasn’t arrived, give it 48 hours — carriers sometimes scan early. Still missing? Contact us and we’ll make it right with a replacement or a refund. If your order arrives damaged, send us a photo and a replacement goes out at no cost to you.
